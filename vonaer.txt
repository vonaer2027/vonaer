vonaer Helicopter Usage Guide

With the vonaer service, booking a helicopter is quick and easy via the app.


Sign Up

Download the vonaer app and complete the personal verification process. Only adults 19 years or older can register.

Select Your Service

For a premium, customized travel experience, choose the VIP charter service, VON Private.

If you want a fast transfer between Gangnam and Incheon Airport, select VON Routine.

To create unforgettable memories in the sky, opt for VON Tour.

Make a Reservation

Choose your preferred location and date, and submit your reservation request.

Reservation Approval and Confirmation

Once an administrator reviews and approves your reservation details, your booking will be confirmed.

Boarding

Arrive at the helipad at your scheduled time, complete a quick boarding procedure, and follow the safety staff’s instructions to board the helicopter.

Disembarkation

After landing, continue your journey comfortably to your final destination with VON RentCar or VON Limousine for your Last Mile transfer.
